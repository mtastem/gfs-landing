Who We Are

Built for the frozen
protein trade.

GFS is a Lebanon-based frozen protein import, export, and supply company operating between international sourcing and local commercial execution. We are not a consumer brand and not a retailer. We are an operator built for disciplined supply.

That means product focus, sourcing awareness, commercial responsiveness, and a cold-chain mindset that professional buyers can actually rely on.

What We Do

Six disciplines.
One platform.

01
Import & Export
Direct sourcing from certified global producers. We manage the full import cycle — origin documentation to Beirut port clearance — and facilitate export to regional trade partners.
02
Protein Sourcing
We locate, qualify, and secure frozen protein from the world's premier producing regions. Our sourcing is driven by quality, Halal compliance, and commercial consistency — not catalogue convenience.
03
Supply & Distribution
Regular supply programs for HORECA groups, wholesale buyers, and trade accounts. Volume flexibility, schedule discipline, and cold-chain accountability at every handoff.
04
Cold-Chain Management
Temperature-controlled storage and logistics from origin to delivery. Every GFS product is maintained within spec. We do not compromise on cold-chain integrity — under any conditions.
05
Trade Partnerships
We partner with international producers, regional importers, and in-market distributors seeking a reliable, commercially serious Lebanese counterpart. Transparent terms. Reliable execution.
06
Commercial Advisory
For buyers entering Lebanon or suppliers navigating trade requirements — sourcing advisory, market intelligence, and introductions to qualified buyers, grounded in real operational experience.
Product Categories

Eight protein categories.

Our portfolio covers the full spectrum of frozen protein — commodity beef to specialty cuts, premium seafood to value-added processing.

01
Beef
Grain-fed, grass-fed, Halal certified. Whole cuts, portions, mince. Brazil, Argentina, Australia.
02
Poultry
Whole birds, IQF portions, breast and thigh cuts. Brazil, Poland, Ukraine origins.
03
Lamb & Mutton
New Zealand and Australian lamb. Whole carcass, bone-in, boneless, rack, leg.
04
Seafood
Wild-caught and farmed. Whole fish, fillets, shrimp, squid. Pacific and Atlantic origins.
05
Veal
European and Australian veal. Premium cuts for fine dining and specialty HORECA accounts.
06
Offal & Variety
Liver, kidney, heart, tripe, tongue. All species. High-volume supply for trade and distribution buyers.
07
Processed & Value-Added
Burgers, sausage, marinated cuts, formed products. Co-packing available. Private label options.
08
Custom Sourcing
Specific species, weight ranges, packaging, or origin requirements. We source to spec — no request too detailed.
